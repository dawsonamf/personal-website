I like building things. While some projects become public, most never leave my machine, and the only people who hear about them are my friends and family. That's what this page is; an excuse to dive deep into something and get it out into the world.

Writing about something is its own kind of project, and so is posting something publicly. It makes you commit to producing a real version of what's in your head, knowing someone might actually look at it. It forces a different kind of clarity than just building alone does.

The topics jump around a lot, but each is crafted with interest, thought, and care. If something kept me up late more than a few nights in a row, it might end up here.
