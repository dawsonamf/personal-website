About Me

I am a recent graduate from the University of Maryland with a dual degree; a B.S. in Computer Science - with a concentration in machine learning - and a B.S. in Finance. I have experience developing across the full application stack, and the full machine learning lifecycle. For more information about my experience, check out my projects below.

I'm interested in building apps that solve real world problems, applied machine learning (especially to finance), and pushing the boundaries of RL algorithms. I love learning, and am always looking for opportunities to develop my skills.

I'm currently working at Visual Language Associates, but am open to the right software engineering opportunity. If you think I'd be a good fit for your team or have any questions at all, feel free to reach out!

Skills

Full Stack

Experienced in both OOP and functional: Python, Matlab, Java, OCaml, Ruby, etc. Familiar with designing and implementing low latency systems architecture with C and C++. Experience with frontend web development with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as some exposure to React and Node.js.

iOS and Mobile

iOS development experience with Swift and SwiftUI. Familiar with frameworks and libraries such as Firebase, CoreData, MusicKit, MapKit, CoreLocation, etc. Some exposure to cross-platform and Android development with Kotlin, Java, and React Native.

ML and RL

Experienced with frameworks such as TensorFlow/Keras, PyTorch, and scikit-learn. I've also developed a variety of ML models from scratch, including neural networks - including backpropagation and gradient descent. I've worked with embeddings, CNNs, autoencoders, and more. For a much more detailed list, check out my projects below.

I've had some exposure to deep reinforcement learning and loved it. I've implemented some of the core algorithms: TD(lambda), DQN and SARSA varieties, some policy gradients, off policy importance sampling, etc. I mainly use OpenAI Gym.
